Description

Abnormality-29 is a noose made from a 3 meter hemp rope. Abnormality-29's peculiarity will only appear when 2 or more people are within 6 meter range from Abnormality-29. When there is only one person within Abnormality-29's range, nothing will happen. When at least 2 people are within Abnormality-29's range, the person entering the range first will be attacked by everyone else within Abnormality-29's range. The attack will not stop until the person who has first entered have left Abnormality-29's range, or until the person dies. If after the first person to enter the range has been eliminated or exited the range, and there are still more than one people within the range, the second person to have entered the range will become the target of attack. People who attacked others within Abnormality-29 will have the memory of doing so, but not remember why they attacked.

Test logs

Test 29-1

Experiment: 2 experimental subjects were ordered to enter the Abnormality-29's range simultaneously.

Result: One of the subjects became the target of attack. Subsequent tests always results with the same subject being the target while the other subject attacks.

Test 29-2

Experiment: Weapons were put on the ground within Abnormality-29's range. 2 experimental subjects were ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range.

Result: The second subject picked up one of the weapons before attacking the first subject. The first subject died.

Test 29-3

Experiment: 3 subjects entered Abnormality-29's range simultaneously.

Result: One of the subjects became the target. After the first target exited Abnormality-29's range, another one of the subject became the target. Subsequent tests shows that the order in which the subjects become the targets are always the same, provided that the used subjects are always the same.

Test 29-4

Experiment: Two experimental subjects were ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range. The second subject's hands were restrained.

Result: The second subject attempted to kill the first subject with headbutts and kicks.

Test 29-5

Experiment: A suicidal experimental subject was ordered to hang himself with Abnormality-29.

Result: Every time subjects attempted to hang himself, the noose became loose enough for his head to go through, causing the subject to fall down and failing to commit suicide.

Test 29-6

Experiment: Two experimental subjects were ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range. The second subject to enter was fully restrained.

Result: The second subject struggled to try to harm the first subject, but failed miserably.

Test 29-7

Experiment: 2 experimental subjects were ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range. The first subject to enter was given Kevlar vests and helmet to wear.

Result: The second subject attempted to harm the first subject, but failed.

Test 29-8

Experiment: Test 29-7 was conducted again. However, a weapon was dropped just outside of Abnormality-29's range.

Result: The second subject left Abnormality-29's range to pick the weapon up, but lost the intent to harm the first subject by the time he got the weapon.

Test 29-9

Experiment: Two chimpanzee were dropped off within Abnormality-29's range.

Result: One of the chimpanzee began attacking the other chimpanzee.

Test 29-10

Experiment: Two chimpanzees were dropped off simultaneously within Abnormality-29.

Result: The test was repeated several times. Both chimpanzees have about 50% chance of becoming the target of attack.

Test 29-11

Experiment: A chimpanzee was dropped off within Abnormality-29's range. Then, an experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range.

Result: The experimental subject attacked the chimpanzee.

Test 29-12

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range. A chimpanzee was dropped off afterwards.

Result: The chimpanzee attacked the subject.

Test 29-13

Experiment: Two plants were dropped off within Abnormality-29's range.

Result: Nothing happened.

Additional notes: Plants have no real way of attacking another creature. Please don't repeat this kind of test where a plant enters last.

Test 29-14

Experiment: A plant was dropped off within Abnormality-29's range. An experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range.

Result: Nothing happened. Subject did not feel anything

Test 29-15

Experiment: A plant was dropped off within Abnormality-29's range. Then a chimpanzee was dropped off within Abnormality-29's range.

Result: Nothing happened. The ape did not attack the plant.

Test 29-16

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to stay within Abnormality-29's range for an indefinite amount of time.

Result: The first few hours, the subject was completely fine. However afterwards, the subject's conditions worsened. The subject became unable to walk and couldn't stand up. Subject's eyesight and hearing became worse. Subject was brought out of the range. Result showed that the subject's immune system attacked its own cells. Other cells also became aggressive and attacked one another. Subject was saved from death, but suffered permanent damage.

Test 29-17

Experiment: A test subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range. No help is given to the subject when her condition worsens.

Result: Subject's condition slowly worsens. Eventually, the subject died after 14 hours. Subject died slowly and excruciatingly.

Test 29-18

Experiment: A caged rat was dropped off within Abnormality-29's range.

Result: The rat's condition worsened faster than humans would. The rat died within 3 hours. Cause of death was confirmed to be due to be the same as test 29-17.

Test 29-19

Experiment: A plant was put within Abnormality-29's range.

Result: The plant took 33 hours to die.

Test 29-20

Experiment: Two subjects were ordered to enter Abnormality-29's range. Subject could not leave the entity’s range.

Result: The first subject attacked the second subject. The second subject retaliated in response. The second subject died, and the first subject realized what he had done. Subject said he felt guilty for murdering someone for no reason.

Test 29-21

Experiment: Fermentation bacteria are put within Abnormality-29's range.

Result: The bacteria died within one minute.

Test 29-22

Experiment: A cat and its kitten is dropped within Abnormality-29.

Result: The cat killed its own kitten.

Test 29-23

Experiment: Abnormality-33 and an experimental subject was put inside Abnormality-29’s range.

Result: Regardless of the order in which they enter, Abnormality-33 and the subject never becomes hostile with one another.

Test 29-24

Experiment: A lion cub was put within Abnormality-29’s range. The lion cub’s mother was put within Abnormality-29’s range after.

Result: The cub attempted to fight back against its own mother, but failed. The lion cub eventually died due to its mother.

Addenda

Test 29-1

Whenever two or more people enters within Abnormality-29's range, one of them will be selected as the target. If it is conducted repeatedly with the same people, the person to become the target will always be the same. There seems to be a pattern to the chosen target when two people enter Abnormality-29's range simultaneously, but it is still unclear. The attacking people inside Abnormality-29's range will pick up weapons if they are available on the ground. Restraints have no effect on attacking people within Abnormality-29's range. They will try to attack the target regardless.

Test 29-2

Abnormality-29 cannot be used for suicide. Any attempt to use Abnormality-29 will be a definite failure. If the attacker within Abnormality-29's range is unable to harm the target, the attacker will attempt to use weapons or other things in the immediate surrounding. However, if the in the process of obtaining weapons, the attacker leaves Abnormality-29's range, the attacker will lose the will to attack the target.

Test 29-3

Abnormality-29 can also influence animals aside from humans. Chimpanzees will attack one another when more than one of them are within Abnormality-29's range. However, repeated tests have shown that both of them has a chance of becoming the target, unlike when humans simultaneously enter the range. Humans may attack animals and vice versa within Abnormality-29. Plants seem to be unaffected by Abnormality-29. Plants cannot attack and will not become a target within Abnormality-29.

Test 29-4

It was found out that staying within Abnormality-29's range for too long could cause cells in the body to attack one another. The process is slow and painful, and may cause death. The process depends on how big and how many cells the organism has. Bigger organisms take longer to die. Plants can be killed this way, albeit taking more time than animals. Bacteria can be affected by Abnormality-29. It is not yet known if archaebacteria can be killed by Abnormality-29.

Origin

Abnormality-29 was first found within a parliament hall. The incident caused several politicians to attack each other. Police came in to suppress the aggressors, but caused more havoc instead. The incident caused no death, but caused many injuries. The organization was called in to take care of the abnormality. The incident has been covered up as an attempted assassination by terrorists. Security camera has shown that someone was hiding Abnormality-29 in the parliament hall disguised as a security. The person's identity is not yet known at the time of the writing.
