Alias: Omnivore slime

Danger level: 4

Description

Abnormality-10 is a slime with an approximate volume of 27000 litre. Abnormality-10 is sentient but aggressive. Abnormality-10 can eat anything organic regardless of what it is or how large it is. Abnormality-10 can use its shape to fit any hole. Anything with a small gap can be gone through by Abnormality-10. Abnormality-10 is a big eater and requires at least a daily consumption of 20 kilo calories. If it is not met, Abnormality-10 will attempt to break out. Entering Abnormality-10's containment room is strictly forbidden as Abnormality-10 eats humans and will do so if given the opportunity. Food is to be provided to Abnormality-10 everyday until the quota is met to prevent breakout incidents. Abnormality-10 is impervious to conventional weaponry. No known methods of eliminating Abnormality-10 exists. Abnormality-10 never increases its body mass.

Test logs

Test 10-1

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was subjected to freezing temperature.

Result: Abnormality-10's movement was slowed down, but no freezing was recorded.

Test 10-2

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was subjected to scorching heat.

Result: Abnormality-10 became faster and more mobile. No further change was recorded.

Test 10-3

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was subjected to explosion.

Result: Abnormality-10 was scattered, but reformed into a single mass shortly after.

Test 10-4

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was subjected to napalm.

Result: Abnormality-10 could shrug off the fire and move at a quick mobility.

Test 10-5

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was poured acid upon.

Result: Abnormality-10 consumed the acid and was temporarily capable of breaking out via corroding its containment room. See incident 10-1 for explanation.

Test 10-6

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was dropped into a big pool of water.

Result: Abnormality-10 could stay afloat and move underwater, but with its mobility severely hampered. Request to transform Abnormality-10's containment room into a water tank is pending.

Test 10-7

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was fed with poison.

Result: No notable change in Abnormality-10.

Test 10-8

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was electrocuted.

Result: Abnormality-10 had a temporary increase in mobility and speed.

Test 10-9

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was fed artificially made food.

Result: Abnormality-10 ate the food, though it seemed to be hesitating or dislike the food.

Test 10-10

Experiment: It was attempted to crush Abnormality-10 with pistons.

Result: Abnormality-10 couldn't be crushed. See incident 10-2

Test 10-11

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was given wood to eat.

Result: Abnormality-10 could eat wood.

Test 10-12

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was given ice cubes.

Result: Abnormality-10 could not eat the ice cubes.

Test 10-13

Experiment: Abnormality-10 was given processed food (yogurt, chess, etc.)

Result: Nearly another breakout incident. See incident 10-3 for explanation.

Incidents

Incident 10-1

In test 10-5, Abnormality-10 obtained the ability to corrode metals and broke out of containment. Abnormality-10 seemed to be sentient enough to use its newly acquired ability to bypass facility security and even flank sentinels. Abnormality-10 was recaptured after it attacked and destroyed a village. Hereafter, chemicals are forbidden to be used in tests and experiments involving Abnormality-10.

Incident 10-2

Test 10-10 involves crushing Abnormality-10 with a piston. Unfortunately, Abnormality-10 multiplied itself to the point crushing it was impossible. Abnormality-10 increased in mass until the piston broke. Abnormality-10 reverted to its original size and immediately attempted escape, causing casualties and destruction. Requirements for test approval has been ramped up to prevent breakout incidents rooting from experiments.

Incident 10-3

In test 10-13, Abnormality-10 obtained the ability to eat non-organic matters after consuming processed food. The effect caused Abnormality-10 to escape its containment room. Fortunately, Abnormality-10 lost its ability shortly after. Sentinels could contain Abnormality-10 before the entity escaped the facility.

Addenda

Addendum 10-1

Abnormality-10 is completely invulnerable. Methods of elimination such as extreme heat, explosion, fire, freezing temperature, poison, acid, and even electrocution has little to no effect on Abnormality-10. However, a peculiar fact, Abnormality-10 seems to be able to move faster in hot conditions. Being burnt or extreme heat allows Abnormality-10 to move faster, even to the point of being capable of matching a racing car depending on the temperature. Additionally, Abnormality-10 has reduced mobility underwater. Therefore, Abnormality-10's containment room has been transformed to a water tank to help contain Abnormality-10. Lastly, chemicals are forbidden to be used in testing. See incident 10-1 for explanation.

Addendum 10-2

Due to the rising number of incidents caused by testing, all testing for Abnormality-10 has been suspended indefinitely. Abnormality-10 shows the capability of becoming stronger or weaker depending on environmental circumstance such as temperature, volume, etc. Abnormality-10 also shows intelligence to make use of its newly acquired abilities. Furthermore, Abnormality-10's danger level has been changed from level 3 to level 4 due to its unpredictability and its intelligence.
