Alias: Eyeglass of wish

Danger level: 1

Description

Abnormality-22 is a seemingly ordinary eyeglass. However, when worn, Abnormality-22 will show a distorted reality of what the wearing person desires. The eyeglass does not change reality. It only changes the reality seen through the glass. Any reality can be seen through Abnormality-22 if the person has strong enough desire for it. If worn for too long, Abnormality-22 will cause discomfort for the user, eventually causing the user to take off Abnormality-22.

Test logs

Test 22-1

Experiment: A suicidal person was given Abnormality-22 to wear. A hidden camera was installed to record what the suicidal person sees.

Result: Abnormality-22 showed the suicidal person writings telling said person that it's okay to commit suicide.

Test 22-2

Experiment: A depressed person was given Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: Abnormality-22 showed the depressed person words of encouragement and care. The person said after the test that he felt better after wearing the eyeglass.

Test 22-3

Experiment: A terrorist was lent Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: Abnormality-22 showed instances of the terrorist group's victories and death of their enemies.

Test 22-4

Experiment: A group of people with differing prescription glasses were lent Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: Every single one of the people said they could see clearly with Abnormality-22.

Test 22-5

Experiment: An experimental subject who does not need glasses to see clearly was lent Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: The subject said Abnormality-22 was uncomfortable to wear.

Test 22-6

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to wear Abnormality-22 and not to take it off.

Result: The subject complied and wore Abnormality-22. The subject said he started feeling uncomfortable, but nothing too extreme that he really wanted to take the entity off.

Test 22-7

Experiment: Abnormality-22 was lent to a person without any strong desires.

Result: The person didn't see anything distorted.

Test 22-8

Experiment: An ex-stalker was lent Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: The ex-stalker saw the poster of the person he used to stalk.

Test 22-9

Experiment: Abnormality-22 was lent to a blind person to wear.

Result: The blind person could not see anything even though wearing Abnormality-22.

Test 22-10

Experiment: An experimental subject who committed the crime of rape was lent Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: The subject saw nothing through the glasses.

Additional notes: The subject has already reformed himself. Therefore, the subject does not have any strong desires.

Test 22-11

Experiment: A recently blinded person with the strong desire to see again was lent Abnormality-22 to wear.

Result: The person stayed blind when wearing Abnormality-22. The person could not see anything.

Test 22-12

Experiment: An experimental subject who does not need to wear glasses was ordered to wear Abnormality-22 for an indefinite amount of time.

Result: The experimental subject repeatedly complained about it being uncomfortable, but did not take it off. The test continued for several hours and nothing happened.

Test 22-13

Experiment: Abnormality-22 was put on a beach.

Result: Not a single speck of sand stuck on Abnormality-22. Abnormality-22 was absolutely clean.

Test 22-14

Experiment: Dirt was smudged on Abnormality-22.

Result: The dirt stayed on Abnormality-22. However, no dust or dirt other than the one smudged could be detected on Abnormality-22.

Test 22-15

Experiment: Abnormality-22 was dropped in mud and then taken out.

Result: Abnormality-22 was wet, but there was no mud on the entity.

Test 22-16

Experiment: Abnormality-22 was buried in dirt, and then dug out.

Result: Abnormality-22 was clean pristine.

Incidents

Incident 22-1

After test 22-8, the ex-stalker started to stalk his old victim. Watchmen realized it and informed police about it. The stalker was apprehended quickly. In light of the incident, Abnormality-22 is forbidden to be used on potentially dangerous individuals, to prevent more incidents similar in nature from happening.

Addenda

Addendum 22-1

Abnormality-22 will show different things to the wearer depending on the wearer's desires. However, if the desires aren't strong enough, nothing will be shown. Additionally, Abnormality-22 could cause discomfort for people wearing the glasses for too long or for people who do not need glasses but wear Abnormality-22. The discomfort will continue for as long as Abnormality-22 is worn, but nothing goes beyond the small sense of discomfort. Blind people can in no way make use of Abnormality-22. Additionally, it is noticed that dust never sticks on Abnormality-22. Further experiments are planned on Abnormality-22.

Origin

Abnormality-22 was obtained from a man who was boasting online. The man had found Abnormality-22 in his backyard a few days prior. The man tried wearing it and also lent it to other people. After knowing Abnormality-22's peculiarity, the man boasted online about having a magical eyeglass. Due to accounts of friend and family supporting the man's claims, watchmen investigated the man. It was found out that the man was telling the truth. Abnormality-22 was confiscated, and the man was interrogated. The man claimed to know nothing about where the eyeglass was from.
