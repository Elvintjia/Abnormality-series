Description

Abnormality-23 is a free downloadable app in the internet. Abnormality-23 works like a virtual private network. However, its ability reaches abnormal levels. When Abnormality-23 is used, any browsing can never be tracked. IP address and any internet presence will vanish. It becomes absolutely impossible to track the device using this app, even with state-of-the-art technology. It is unknown who or what created Abnormality-23. Abnormality-23 takes zero storage space in the device and only has the simple interface of activation and deactivation. Abnormality-23 is hidden as much as possible from the public as well as other institutions and countries, as the potential for misuse can be high.

Test logs

Test 23-1

Experiment: After browsing with Abnormality-23 activated, browser history would be checked.

Result: Browsing history with Abnormality-23 activated was non-existent.

Test 23-2

Experiment: A phone's navigation app was opened when Abnormality-23 was activated.

Result: Satellite couldn't tell where the phone's location was.

Test 23-3

Experiment: Abnormality-23 was activated where wifi wasn't available.

Result: Nothing happened. Abnormality-23 wouldn't activate.

Test 23-4

Experiment: A comment was made in the internet with a logged in account and Abnormality-23 activated.

Result: On the phone that sent the comment, the account's name can be seen as the commenter. On other devices, the commenter's name is always anonymous.

Test 23-5

Experiment: A hacking was made with Abnormality-23.

Result: The target account was hacked and no trace of tampering could be found.

Test 23-6

Experiment: A virus was downloaded on a hacked device while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: Antivirus could handle the virus, but cannot trace the source of the virus.

Test 23-7

Experiment: An image was uploaded to the internet while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: There was the writing, "Uploaded by anonymous."

Test 23-8

Experiment: Another phone was called while Abnormality-23 was activated.

Result: The caller was displayed as anonymous. The phone number of the caller was 10 zeroes.

Test 23-9

Experiment: A message was sent to another phone while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: The message was received in another conversation. The sender is written as anonymous. No message can be sent to the sender.

Test 23-10

Experiment: A message was sent from one phone to another. Both phones had Abnormality-23 activated.

Result: The receiver phone never received the message, until Abnormality-23 was deactivated.

Test 23-11

Experiment: A pirated content was downloaded while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: The pirated content could be downloaded without interference from antivirus software.

Test 23-12

Experiment: A post in the dark web was made, challenging hackers to hack the phone used. Abnormality-23 was continually activated.

Result: No hacker managed to hack the phone. Hackers claimed the phone used was non-existent.

Test 23-13

Experiment: Abnormality-23 was activated in the middle of a stream.

Result: The stream ended, and another stream with an anonymous account was started. When the stream was ended, the account is nowhere to be found and the stream wasn't archived.

Test 23-14

Experiment: A phone with Abnormality-23 active was used while on a plane.

Result: Nothing happened. Aircraft communication experienced no interference.

Test 23-15

Experiment: An online post was liked while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: The post has one additional like. The like did not disappear even after Abnormality-23 was inactivated.

Test 23-16

Experiment: A phone was restarted while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: When the phone had rebooted, Abnormality-23 was still active.

Test 23-17

Experiment: An online game was played while Abnormality-23 was active.

Result: The online account was had the username anonymous, cannot be chatted with, and cannot be reported. The game publishers attempt to ban the account resulted in errors.

Incidents

Incident 23-1

Staff member Stefan activated Abnormality-23 while surfing the deep web to collect information. However, Staff member Stefan has left Abnormality-23 active and put his phone away. Later on, it was unknown where the phone had gone to, as calling the phone did not make it ring. It was found later after a fifteen minute search. Staff members are advised never to leave Abnormality-23 activated when the phone is not in use.

Incident 23-2

Staff member Bernard turned on Abnormality-23 while surfing the web. The company owning the website he surfed at found out his suspicious activities because his surfing does not leave any cookies or any data. The company attempted to ban him, but it instead caused an error. After hours of attempts, the company gave up. The company informed other companies of a user with no cookies or data that can be collected. It became a huge commotion amongst digital security companies and web companies. After realizing the mistake, the organization went and cleared the misunderstanding. Abnormality-23 is no longer allowed to be used for personal use.

Addenda

Addendum 23-1

After series of tests, it was found out that devices with Abnormality-23 activated cannot in any way be tracked. All account used when Abnormality-23 is active will be replaced with a non-existent anonymous account. Hackers or web companies are completely unable to track or detect the account. Permission has been given for Abnormality-23 to be used when gathering information in the web or hacking, as it is a very effective VPN.

Addendum 23-2

In light of incident 23-1, all personnel are advised never to leave Abnormality-23 active when their device isn't being used. This is because all communication to the device becomes impossible when Abnormality-23 is active. All communication from a device when Abnormality-23 is active will always be replaced with an anonymous. Additionally, devices with Abnormality-23 active do not emit radio signals. Phones can be used on aeroplanes without any problem so long as Abnormality-23 is active.
