Abnormality-25

Alias: Painting of the worlds

Danger level: 4

Table of contents

  1. Description
  2. Test logs
  3. Addenda
  4. Origin

Description

Abnormality-25 is a seemingly blank canvas painting with only one color on its canvas, blue. Abnormality-25 is 40 cm in width and 60 cm in height. Abnormality-25 does not do anything, but can cause problems in its own way. Creatures such as animals and even abnormalities can appear from the inside of Abnormality-25. The creature is usually in good health and is slightly stronger and more durable than their real world counterparts. The creatures are always aggressive and will sought to kill any other living things they can kill in the vicinity. The creatures often cause problems and sometimes also cause breakout incidents. No known methods is capable of preventing Abnormality-24 from summoning the creatures. Platoons of commandos and sentinels are always on standby in the case a dangerous creature were to appear from Abnormality-25.

Test logs

Test 25-1

Experiment: Abnormality-25 was set on fire.

Result: Abnormality-25 stayed undamaged despite being engulfed in flames.

Test 25-2

Experiment: Abnormality-25 was squished with 1000 N worth of pressure.

Result: The entity was not damaged in any way.

Test 25-3

Experiment: Abnormality-25 was subjected to explosion.

Result: Abnormality-25 stayed undamaged.

Test 25-4

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to try to enter Abnormality-25.

Result: Experimental subject managed to enter Abnormality-25. Subject was not equipped for expedition, so subject was called back.

Test 25-4A

Experiment: The experimental subject was reequipped with expedition supplies and tools. The subject was ordered to reenter Abnormality-25.

Result: The subject travelled for a few meters. Communication device still worked within Abnormality-25. However, video cameras are immediately disabled upon entering. The subject described the inside of Abnormality-25 as a surreal strange thing. Subject claimed to have been to an art gallery, a bedroom, a palace, and a library just by roaming around. The subject then claimed to encounter some kind of dinosaur. Communication was then lost with the subject.

Test 25-5

Experiment: A second expedition was sent. Another experimental subject was sent. A handgun was provided for self-defense.

Result: Experimental subject couldn't enter Abnormality-25, even though the previous subject didn't have any problems.

Test 25-5A

Experiment: The experimental subject was ordered to enter once again, but without the pistol.

Result: Experimental subject still couldn't enter Abnormality-25.

Test 25-5B

Experiment: Another experimental subject was ordered to do the expedition instead. The experimental subject was given a pistol for self-defense.

Result: Experimental subject could enter Abnormality-25. Experimental subject said he had been to a pool, a mountain, and an office. The subject then yelled, claiming to have seen a some tigers. Shots were fired. Subject requested permission to return. Permission was granted. The subject returned a few minutes after. All the bullets in the pistol had been emptied. Subject claimed to have shot a tiger several times, yet the tiger did not die. The tiger died only after all bullets had been unloaded on a single tiger. The subject then escaped to run for his own life back to the real world.

Test 25-6

Experiment: Another expedition was sent into Abnormality-25. A digital camera was brought to take photos.

Result: Experimental subject was ordered to walk around inside Abnormality-25 for a few minutes and taking a few photos before returning. All the photos taken were blurry. The subject claimed that he did everything possible, but the photos were always blurry no matter what.

Test 25-7

Experiment: A group of commandos with advanced weapon was sent to explore into Abnormality-25.

Result: The commandos had visited a bunker, a wasteland, a tower, and a factory. Most buildings had creatures. No writing or text was found. The commandos encountered an elephant and engaged it. Three commandos were lost. The commandos were given the orders to take photos and pull back. Part of the elephant's body was brought back.

Test 25-8

Experiment: Another expedition was sent to Abnormality-25. Experimental subject was ordered to collect dirt samples.

Result: Subject successfully acquired sand and dirt samples from a beach and a forest respectively. The subject encountered several aggressive birds on the return trip. Subject managed to return, though injured. Subject's left hand had to be amputated.

Addenda

Addendum 25-1

It has been found out that a world exist within Abnormality-25. The world in Abnormality-25 is largely bizarre and changes every time it is entered. The world is largely empty, yet resembles Earth. Occasionally, animals can be encountered. The animals encountered are as tough as animals that sometimes appear from Abnormality-25. Communication equipment somehow works while within Abnormality-25. Any video recording equipment inside Abnormality-25 will deactivate itself. Photos taken within Abnormality-25 will always be blurry. Analysis of the dirt and sand collected from Abnormality-5 is still ongoing.

Origin

Abnormality-25 was found in an art gallery, replacing one of the paintings. A creature came out just as the art gallery was about to be opened. The unit were sent to eliminate the creatures and deal with the mess. No information regarding how Abnormality-25 got to the gallery is known. Surviving staff member of the art gallery admitted to seeing the painting, but not knowing if it was supposed to be on display. The unit attempted to destroy Abnormality-25 by many means, including but not limited to burning and tearing. None of the attempts succeeded. Abnormality-25 was to be handed to the local government, but the local government was unwilling to take in such a dangerous abnormality. Hence, Abnormality-25 was given to the organization.
