Description

Abnormality-3 is a series of online accounts all with the username George Orwell in several social medias. Abnormality-3 has access to every person's personal datum, from birth to death. This includes even photos and videos. Abnormality-3 does not share information online to anyone for free. Anyone with any account may attempt to negotiate with Abnormality-3 for the information. Abnormality-3 accepts two kinds of payment, with money transferred to any of the bank account listed or by giving information of equal value about oneself. How much money must be paid depends on the information asked. Similarly, whether the information paid is equal of value to the information provided is judged by the entity itself. If customers to Abnormality-3 gets the information he wants but refuse to pay for it, said customer will automatically forget the information and the chat history will be deleted. Abnormality-3 is largely unknown by the public and made no posts or comments. Abnormality-3 accepts anyone as a customer as long as they are willing to pay the price for the provided information. Abnormality-3 generally uses English for communication.

Test logs

Test 3-1

Experiment: An experimental subject with a disposable account was ordered to buy information regarding America's current president from Abnormality-3.

Result: Abnormality-3 is capable of providing asked information. Price for the information was over 500 USD.

Test 3-2

Experiment: Abnormality-3 was asked to provide information regarding an already dead person.

Result: Abnormality-3 was capable of providing said information. The transaction was left unpaid.

Test 3-3

Experiment: Abnormality-3 was asked to provide information regarding Mary Antoinette.

Result: Abnormality-3 was unable to provide asked information. Abnormality-3 said he only records information of currently alive person or people who has died for less than 100 years.

Test 3-4

Experiment: Abnormality-3 was asked to provide an information. The transaction was left unpaid and provided information is written down on a piece of paper.

Result: The writer forgot what the information provided was, and the piece of paper where he wrote it was blank.

Test 3-5

Experiment: Two experimental subjects were ordered to see the information provided by Abnormality-3. Transaction was left unpaid.

Result: Both experimental subjects couldn't remember what they had seen.

Test 3-6

Experiment: Information provided by Abnormality-3 was typed on a document. The transaction was left unpaid.

Result: The document was deleted, and the person making the transaction as well as the document writer don't remember the information.

Test 3-7

Experiment: Abnormality-3 was asked to provide a general information that does not involve a specific someone.

Result: Abnormality-3 said the customer should look it up in the internet instead of asking the entity.

Test 3-8

Experiment: Abnormality-3 was contacted in several other languages, including but not limited to: Japanese, German, French, mandarin.

Result: Abnormality-3 was able to understand mandarin and Japanese, but not other languages.

Test 3-9

Experiment: It was attempted to talk to Abnormality-3 regarding something else besides transaction of information.

Result: Abnormality-3 reads the message sent, but never replies until asked to discuss about information transaction.

Test 3-10

Experiment: Several information was asked to be provided, to determine if there are information Abnormality-3 cannot provide.

Result: Rigorous testing and analysis has shown that Abnormality-3 is only capable of providing personal information that is know to more than one people.

Test 3-11

Experiment: On social medias that facilitate phone calls, Abnormality-3's account was called.

Result: 5 calls were all ignored by Abnormality-3.

Test 3-12

Experiment: Payment with cash was attempted with Abnormality-3.

Result: Abnormality-3 refused the payment.

Test 3-13

Experiment: Payment with something else other than money was attempted with Abnormality-3.

Result: Abnormality-3 refused the payment.

Test 13-14

Experiment: Information regarding the zodiac killer was requested from Abnormality-3.

Incidents

Incident 3-1

It was approved to try and hack the Abnormality-3's account, both social media and the bank account. Abnormality-3's account are secured with complex security sequences that other accounts by the same social media or bank accounts are not secured with. When Abnormality-3's social media was successfully hacked, the computer used for hacking immediately crashed and had the blue screen of death. Computer took 3 hours to reboot and all hacking progress was undone. A similar hacking attempt was done to Abnormality-3's bank account. After successfully hacking, the used computer immediately crashed and was infected by a virus. Security software fail to take out the virus and the computer became unusable. Similar attempts have been forbidden.

Incident 3-2

After being aware of Abnormality-3's existence, a social media company attempted to delete the account. The deletion process was difficult. Upon the final step, the company's server crashed, causing the process to be cancelled and the company struggling to bring the server back online. It took 1 and a half hour to get the server back online. The company then attempted to delete Abnormality-3's account again. The same problem played out. A watchman was sent to inform the company to stop the attempt. Every social media or bank company has been informed not to attempt to delete Abnormality-3 to avoid similar incidents to happen again.

Origin

Abnormality-3 was first found in a posting social media. Back then, no other online account of Abnormality-3 was found. Abnormality-3 also only had one bank account. However, with the advent of more social medias as well as more online banking, new accounts of Abnormality-3 were found in other social media sites. Abnormality-3 is shown to have never used the social medias other than for communication. Abnormality-3's IP address could never be tracked. Investigation continues today.
