Abnormality-8

Alias: Favor telephone

Danger level: 1

Description

Abnormality-8 is a 2008 blue-colored telephone. Abnormality-8 looks like a normal telephone at a glance. Abnormality-8 cannot be used to call people. The number to call Abnormality-8 is not known, up until now. Occasionally, Abnormality-8 will ring. If Abnormality-8 is answered, a voice will speak and ask for a favour. The favour can range from something simple like telling what is 12 times 18, or something absurd like asking to talk to a specific person whose location is completely unknown. If the favour is declined, the voice will hang up. If the favour is accepted, the person accepting the favour must complete the favour within 20 hours. If the twenty hours limit is up but the favour is not done yet, the person accepting will lose one of these: Sense of sight, sense of sound, sense of smell, or sense of taste. There are no clear pattern which sensory will be lost. If the favour has been completed, Abnormality-8 will ring again. If the same person picks up the phone, the voice will congratulate the person and reward a gift to said person. Said person will get a random gift, ranging from useless items, to items said person desperately needs. The gift will always appear next to Abnormality-8 and is never harmful or dangerous. Abnormality-8 does not need electricity to ring, but will typically give easier favors when it is connected to electricity.

Test logs

Test 8-1

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to ask the voice in Abnormality-8 several questions

Result: Once the voice couldn't get a yes as an answer after asking the experimental subject thrice for a favor, it hanged up. Experimental subject said the voice didn't answer his question no matter how he asked.

Test 8-2

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to answer Abnormality-8. The subject was ordered to ask the voice to make the favor easier or change the favor.

Result: The voice was willing to change or make the favor easier by making a new offer up to three times, before hanging up because of not getting a yes answer.

Test 8-3

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to ask for a specific reward from Abnormality-8 for completing the task he had given.

Result: Abnormality-8 was willing to give a specific reward, but said the greater the reward, the greater the favor. After the subject did the favor, Abnormality-8 congratulated the subject and the promised reward appeared.

Result: After his offer was refused thrice, the voice hanged up.

Test 8-5

Result: After three times of telling the subject that he shouldn't ask for a heavier offer, the voice gave up and gave a favor heavier than the reward is worth. After the favor had been completed and the subject had talked to the voice a second time, the asked reward plus one bonus reward appeared next to Abnormality-8.

Addenda

Addendum 8-1

For some unknown reason, the voice in Abnormality-8 uses three as a basis number of everything. The voice is willing to give a specific reward if asked for, but will always give a favor of equal value to the asked reward. The specific reward will then appear after the favor is done and the voice have congratulated the asking person. If insisted thrice to be given a heavier favor than the reward is worth, the voice will give in and give a favor with heavier worth than the reward. When the reward is received, an additional reward will also appear for the asking person. In addition, the favor's reward can never expire. The reward will always be available after the favor is done. The voice seems to only be capable of giving material rewards.

Origin

Abnormality-8 was first found in a civilian's house. Watchmen found out about it and Abnormality-8 was secured. The family living in the house claimed they did not know how or why the abnormality came to be. The mother of the family claimed she had bought the telephone from a store nearby and even had the receipt. The receipt was investigated, and it was a genuine one. However, upon visit to the store where Abnormality-8 was "bought" from, the staff claimed they never saw the mother of the family and the store didn't sell telephones. Camera showed that the mother of family indeed never visited the store and there was really no telephone sold in the store.
