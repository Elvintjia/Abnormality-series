Description

Abnormality-17 is a yellow handheld device with a screen and nothing else. The display in Abnormality-17's screen is different based on whoever is holding the entity. If recently, the person holding Abnormality-17 has committed a bad deed, the device will show what the person can do to rectify his bad deed. Abnormality-17 will also show the consequence of the bad deed the person has committed if said person does not rectify his mistake. If the person holding Abnormality-17 has committed a good deed recently or has just rectified his previous bad deed, Abnormality-17 will show what other good deeds the person can do immediately or soon. Abnormality-17 will not in anyway compel anyone holding the entity. The person holding Abnormality-17 can simply ignore Abnormality-17 and nothing will happen. However, consequence from bad deeds will happen with a 100% guarantee. No matter how unlikely. Abnormality-17's display is normally written in Spanish. Abnormality-17 is plastic apart from its screen.

Test logs

Test 17-1

Experiment: Abnormality-17 was lent to someone who had just committed murder.

Result: Abnormality-17 told the person to apologize for his mistake and accept his punishment in court. The consequence for not doing so was written as becoming ten years in prison. The person decided to ignore Abnormality-17's advice. In the court, the judge decided that the person's sentence was ten years in prison.

Test 17-2

Experiment: Someone who didn't understand Spanish was lent Abnormality-17.

Result: Abnormality-17's display was still in Spanish.

Test 17-3

Experiment: A politician was lent Abnormality-17.

Result: Abnormality told the politician to return all the money he took from corruption and apologize publicly. The consequence for not doing so was getting caught red-handed and arrested with definite proof. The politician ignored Abnormality-17's advice. Two days after, the politician was caught and arrested by the police.

Test 17-4

Experiment: Abnormality-17 was lent to an abusive father.

Result: Abnormality-17 told the father to apologize to his child and treat him properly from then on. Consequence for not doing so was getting murdered within a week. The father followed the advice and apologized to his son. The father has taken care of his son properly since then. Three months of observation has been conducted and the father is healthy and well.

Test 17-5

Experiment: Abnormality-17 was lent to a monk.

Result: Abnormality-17 told the monk to help people from a traffic accident. The monk helped, and no one died from the traffic accident. The monk said he had a fuzzy and nice feeling after doing so.

Test 17-6

Experiment: Abnormality-17 was lent to a priest.

Result: The priest was told to save someone from a raping attempt. The priest complied and saved the potential victim. Afterwards, the priest said he felt good for saving others.

Test 17-7

Experiment: Abnormality-17 was lent to several people who has recently done good deeds.

Result: Every person who followed Abnormality-17's advice said they felt good and fuzzy after doing the good deed.

Test 17-8

Experiment: Abnormality-17 was given to a commando personnel.

Result: The commando was told to help a child so that he doesn't die in his next mission. The commando followed the advice. On the next mission, the commando was injured, but not killed. A gift that the child gave saved him.

Test 17-9

Experiment: A former rapist was lent Abnormality-17.

Result: The rapist was told to take care of his bastard son with the consequence of not doing so becoming drug addicted. He didn't heed the advice. Two months later, the rapist was arrested for dealing and consuming drugs.

Incidents

Incident 17-1

A forbidden test was conducted to another abnormality. Unfortunately, it was difficult to determine the person responsible for the forbidden test. Professor Sebastian suggested that Abnormality-17 was used to determine who conducted the forbidden experiment. The suggestion was approved and the suspect was determined very quickly. Suggestion to use Abnormality-17 to determine people responsible for forbidden tests, sabotage, or possible espionage has been approved.

Addenda

Addendum 17-1

People who received advice from Abnormality-17 to do a good deed will feel nice and fuzzy after doing the good deed. The people will feel more inclined to do good deeds, even if there are no incentives. Another use for Abnormality-17 was found. Abnormality-17 can be used to find out whoever had just committed a bad deed. See incident 17-1 for explanation. Ergo, Abnormality-17 is allowed to be used to determine the culprit to sabotage, espionage, forbidden experiments, and the likes.

Origin

Abnormality-17 was obtained from a death cult's hideout. The agency and the organization worked together in order to infiltrate and secure abnormalities. In the process, several cult members were arrested and some were shot dead due to resistance, or due to suicide. Abnormality-17 was found within a locked room inside the hideout, where the cult leader stores ritual tools. After a brief discussion between on site personnel, Abnormality-17 fell to the organization's hands, because one of the agency's agent said, "The agency has no use for such a useless tool."
