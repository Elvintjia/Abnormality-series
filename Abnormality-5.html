Description

Abnormality-5 is an antique gramophone from 19th century. Abnormality-5 can play music even without a vinyl record. The music played will change every time with no clear pattern as to which song will be played. Anyone listening to the music will be inclined to stay and listen to the music. Anyone listening to the music will not willingly stop listening to the music unless interrupted or the music stops playing. First ten minutes of listening has no effect on the people listening. Ten minutes to twenty minutes of listening will cause listeners to refuse to stop listening unless forced by others or emergencies happening. Twenty minutes to thirty minutes, listeners cannot be stopped from listening to Abnormality-5 even after being forced by others. More than thirty minutes of listening will cause listeners to be inclined to stop listening to the music and leave. Listeners who have listened to Abnormality-5 will henceforth be referred to as Abnormality-5A. If Abnormality-5A listen to any song, Abnormality-5A will consider the songs bad or tone deaf, even to songs they previously liked. The abnormal effects on Abnormality-5A has never faded and to date, no Abnormality-5A have lost their inability to enjoy music.

Test logs

Test 5-1

Experiment: Abnormality-5 was played continuously without stopping. No listeners were present.

Result: Abnormality-5 played song up to thirty five hours and still did not stop. Staff members had to manually stop Abnormality-5

Test 5-2

Experiment: Music played from Abnormality-5 was recorded. Recording was played to an experimental subject.

Result: Experimental subject showed no sign of being inclined to listen to the song continuously, and was still able to enjoy other music.

Test 5-3

Experiment: An Abnormality-5A was ordered to listen to songs played by Abnormality-5.

Result: Abnormality-5A remarked that the song from Abnormality-5A was good music, but doesn't feel inclined to simply continue listening to it.

Test 5-4

Experiment: Abnormality-5A was asked to listen to the same song he heard when he listened to Abnormality-5.

Result: Abnormality-5A remarked that the music is bad and tone deaf.

Test 5-5

Experiment: An experimental subject capable of playing music was asked to listen to Abnormality-5. Said experimental subject, now Abnormality-5A, was asked to play music on an instrument.

Result: Abnormality-5A could still play music, but remarked that he didn't feel satisfied with the result.

Test 5-6

Experiment: Abnormality-5 was played for an animal to listen to. Another song from an MP3 was then played for said animal to listen.

Result: The cat used as test subject scratched the MP3 player until it broke.

Test 5-7

Experiment: A microphone was put near Abnormality-5. Abnormality-5 was then played. A room filled with 5 experimental subjects had a speaker connected to the microphone.

Result: All experimental subjects think that the music played was great, but developed no symptoms that Abnormality-5A have.

Test 5-8

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5. After twenty minutes of listening, sentinels would try to get the experimental subject to stop listening to Abnormality-5.

Result: Experimental subject showed super strength and was capable of overpowering sentinels. When the music from Abnormality-5 was stopped, experimental subject suddenly stopped attacking sentinels and lost his super strength. Post-experiment doctor analysis showed experimental subject was but a normal human with nothing noteworthy.

Test 5-9

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5. After twenty minutes of listening, sentinels would try to get the experimental subject to stop listening to Abnormality-5.

Result: Experimental subject refused to leave, but it was still possible to forcefully drag the experimental subject out of the room and stop listening to the music. No superhuman strength was recorded.

Test 5-10

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5 for one hour.

Result: After thirty two minutes of listening to the music, the experimental subject, now Abnormality-5A, stopped Abnormality-5A's music.

Test 5-11

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5 for an hour. Stopping the music midway was forbidden.

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5 for an hour. Stopping the music midway was forbidden.

Test 5-12

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5. Said experimental subject was restrained to prevent action.

Result: After listening for thirty minutes, experimental subject, now Abnormality-5A, starts struggling, but to no avail. Forty minutes after first listening, Abnormality-5A screamed and begged to be freed, or for the music to be stopped. Abnormality-5A was freed and the music was stopped. Abnormality-5A refused to listen to any music, stating that it is going to bring about trauma.

Test 5-13

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5. Said experimental subject was restrained to prevent action.

Result: Abnormality-5A destroyed the speaker with punches and kicks. No superhuman strength was recorded.

Test 5-14

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5's music. The experimental subject was restrained.

Result: Experimental subject, now Abnormality-5A had similar reaction to Abnormality-5A in test 5-12. After fifty minutes of listening to the Abnormality-5A's music, Abnormality-5A became hysterical and uttered, "Kill me." Abnormality-5's music was stopped. Abnormality-5A has become deaf and unable to hear.

Test 5-15

Experiment: Abnormality-5A from test 5-12 was subjected to Abnormality-5's music.

Result: Said Abnormality-5 hit his head to the wall several times while listening to Abnormality-5's music. Experiment was aborted and music from Abnormality-5 was stopped. Abnormality-5's head is injured, but no change in abnormal symptoms.

Test 5-16

Experiment: Abnormality-5A from test 5-14 was subjected to Abnormality-5's music.

Result: Abnormality-5A suddenly froze and stopped breathing. Music was stopped, but Abnormality-5A continued not to breathe. When touched or pushed, Abnormality-5A was like a ragdoll and does not respond to it. Communication attempts also failed. Three minutes after, Abnormality-5A died due to lack of oxygen.

Test 5-17

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to listen to Abnormality-5's music. The experimental subject was restrained and any plea to stop the song was denied.

Result: After sixty minutes of listening to Abnormality-5's music, experimental subject, now Abnormality-5A, started bleeding from his eyes, ears, nose, and mouth. Five minutes of bleeding later, Abnormality-5A died due to blood loss.

Test 5-18

Experiment: Abnormality-5A from test 5-12 was forced to listen to Abnormality-5's music.

Result: Abnormality-5A banged his head to the wall until he died due to brain and skull damage.

Test 5-19

Experiment: An experimental subject was subjected to similar processes as test 5-12. Said experimental subject, now Abnormality-5A was restrained and forced to listen to Abnormality-5's music.

Result: After ten minutes of listening, Abnormality-5A started bleeding from his face similar to Abnormality-5A in test 5-17, and died shortly after.

Test 5-20

Experiment: An experimental subject was transformed into Abnormality-5A through the processes of test 5-12. Abnormality-5A was then subjected to Abnormality-5's music, but Abnormality-5A was given earmuff for him to use.

Result: Abnormality-5A could still hear Abnormality-5's music and similarly, banged his head on the wall.

Test 5-21

Experiment: Abnormality-5A with thirty minutes of exposure was given earmuffs to use while Abnormality-5's music was played.

Result: Abnormality-5A remarked he could not hear any music.

Test 5-22

Experiment: Abnormality-5A with thirty minutes of exposure was subjected to listening to Abnormality-5's music.

Result: Abnormality-5A listened to Abnormality-5's music for two hours before staff members decided to stop the music. Abnormality-5A did not resist or have any inclination to continue listening to the music. Abnormality-5A remarked it was good music.

Test 5-23

Experiment: An experimental subject was subjected to Abnormality-5's music for twenty minutes. Sentinels would then try to kill said experimental subject with bullets and guns.

Result: Experimental subject died. No superpowers were recorded.

Test 5-24

Experiment: An experimental subject was exposed to Abnormality-5's music for fifty minutes. Said subject would then be given earmuffs then continued listening to Abnormality-5's music.

Result: Abnormality-5A stopped breathing and died shortly after.

Incidents

Incident 5-1

Staff member Diana's earmuffs were not worn properly. As such, staff member Diana became entranced with Abnormality-5's song. Staff member Diana took off her earmuffs and listened to the song. Other staff members entered to get staff member Diana out. Unfortunately, their attempts were in vain as staff member Diana insisted on staying. Sentinels were called to forcefully get staff member Diana out. Even sentinels were unable to force her to stop listening. Staff member Diana showed impressive strength exceeding that of an average human. Sentinels dragging her away was thrown across the room. One sentinel accidentally hit Abnormality-5, causing the gramophone to stop playing. Immediately after, staff member Diana stopped struggling and realized what she was doing. She apologized and willingly took punishment for the incident. Later, doctor's diagnosis showed nothing wrong with staff member Diana, even though Diana seemingly had super strength. Staff member Diana also retained her ability to continue enjoying music and didn't become Abnormality-5A. Further investigation needs to be done to confirm super strength effects of Abnormality-5 on humans.

Incident 5-2

In the preparation for test 5-18, Abnormality-5A stole an earmuff from a staff member whilst another staff member activated Abnormality-5's music. Abnormality-5A claimed he could still hear the music despite wearing the earmuffs. Abnormality-5A was frightened and started crying, begging for the music to stop. The music was stopped and Abnormality-5A was crying on the floor. Test 5-18 proceeded, despite Abnormality-5A's cry and plea.

Addenda

Addendum 5-1

Tests so far have indicated that Abnormality-5 will force people to continue listening at the music once said people have listened more than twenty minutes. People who have listened more than twenty minutes will gain superpowers to continue listening. Abnormality-5A can still enjoy music from Abnormality-5, but not from other sources. Test to try and see what happens when a person listens to Abnormality-5's music more than thirty minutes are currently pending. Abnormality-5's music can be listened to indirectly through speakers without having to worry of side effects. Abnormality-5's music can also influence animals as effectively as humans are.

Addendum 5-2

After a series of tests, it has been determined that listening to Abnormality-5's music more than thirty minutes is very dangerous. Forty minutes of listening will cause Abnormality-5A to hate music and destroy music sources whenever possible. If not possible, Abnormality-5A will bang his head to the wall. Peculiarly, Abnormality-5A won't bother to turn off Abnormality-5's music, even if it's possible and not forbidden. Listening for fifty minutes will cause Abnormality-5A to become deaf, unable to hear anything. Exposing said Abnormality-5A to Abnormality-5's music will cause Abnormality-5A to stop breathing and die shortly after, even if the music is stopped before Abnormality-5A dies. Exposure to Abnormality-5A's music for more than sixty minutes will cause Abnormality-5A to lose blood from the eyes, ears, mouth, and nose. Stopping Abnormality-5's music will not stop the bleeding.

Addendum 5-3

With earmuffs, any human and Abnormality-5A with thirty minutes of exposure cannot hear Abnormality-5's music. However, Abnormality-5A's with forty minutes of exposure or more seem to be able to hear and are still influenced by the music even with earmuffs. Abnormality-5A with thirty minutes of exposure also can listen to Abnormality-5's music without any notable consequence.
