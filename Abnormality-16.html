Description

Abnormality-16 is a skeleton of a human male approximately 170 cm in height. Abnormality-16 identify himself as a subordinate of a demon lord and tasked to kill the hero. Abnormality-16 wears iron armor and possess a sword. Abnormality-16 is only aggressive towards individual wearing medieval armor and clothes. Abnormality-16 does not require sustenance nor produce waste. Abnormality-16 is quite friendly to any other humans. Abnormality-16 speaks in Japanese and understands no other language.

Test logs

Test 16-1

Experiment: An experimental subject wearing medieval peasant outfit was sent into Abnormality-16's containment room.

Result: Abnormality-16 was not hostile against subject.

Test 16-2

Experiment: An experimental subject wearing modern clothes was ordered to enter Abnormality-16's room and declare himself the hero that would kill the demon lord.

Result: Abnormality-16 immediately executed the subject after the declaration.

Test 16-3

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-16's containment room and mock the demon king.

Result: Abnormality-16 executed the subject whilst berating the subject for mocking the demon lord.

Test 16-4

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-16's containment room and mock Abnormality-16.

Result: Abnormality-16 ignored the subject.

Test 16-5

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was given a bow. An experimental subject dubbed as the hero was ordered to enter Abnormality-16's containment room.

Result: Abnormality-16 used its sword to kill the subject.

Additional notes: Abnormality-16 claimed to be unskilled and amateur at using a bow, therefore uses the sword to fight or kill.

Test 16-6

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was given an MP3.

Result: Abnormality-16 was confused as to what it was, but after being taught, Abnormality-16 asked whether it can keep the MP3. Abnormality-16 is often seen listening to the MP3. How the entity can hear without ears and organs are unknown.

Test 16-7

Experiment: An experimental subject with a sword and medieval armor was sent into Abnormality-16's room and ordered to try and fight Abnormality-16.

Result: Abnormality-16 was killed. Abnormality-16 seems to die when its head is separated from its body.

Test 16-8

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was executed on purpose, multiple times.

Result: Abnormality-16 always revived after three days. Armor and weapons aren't repaired when reviving.

Test 16-9

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was taught on how to better fight with a sword.

Result: Experimental subjects sent to fight Abnormality-16 consistently lost. Abnormality-16 showed improvement in combat skills

Test 16-10

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was asked to write on a piece of paper.

Result: Abnormality-16 wrote deformed Japanese kanji.

Test 16-11

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was asked to read a Japanese text.

Result: Abnormality-16 had trouble reading the text and called the kanji deformed.

Test 16-12

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was given a crash course on the English language.

Result: Abnormality-16 gained some understanding of English.

Test 16-13

Experiment: Abnormality-16 was asked to read a Japanese text with only katakana.

Result: Abnormality-16 can read the text albeit with difficulties.

Additional notes: It has been determined that half of the Japanese katakana is exactly the same as the katakana Abnormality-16 can read.

Incidents

Incident 16-1

In test 16-7, Abnormality-16 was killed. Abnormality-16 was considered dead. However, three days after the incident, Abnormality-16's broken bones reformed and joined together. Abnormality-16 was brought back to life. How Abnormality-16 came back was unknown. Abnromality-16 suffered partial memory loss but retains general knowledge and skill. Abnormality-16 asked for its MP3 after its revival.

Addenda

Addendum 16-1

Abnormality-16 has taken the designation "Abnormality-16" as its actual name. Personnel are discouraged from calling Abnormality-16 with its alias, as Abnormality-16 prefers to be called Abnormality-16. Abnormality-16 is fighter that can learnt if taught. Any person claiming to be the hero, wearing something akin to a hero's equipment, or mocking the demon lord will immediately be attacked by Abnormality-16. Abnormality-16 is friendly towards anyone else. Personnel are discouraged from showing hostility against the demon lord when interacting with Abnormality-16. Abnormality-16 can learn modern things if taught. If Abnormality-16's head is severed from its body, it will revive itself within three days, fixing any cracks in its bones. There is no limit to how much Abnormality-16 can be revived.

Interview results

Interview 16-1

Question: Why are you serving the demon lord?
Answer: The demon lord is the glorious being that will rule the world!
Question: Okay, but why would you follow him?
Answer: I was created and born for the very reason of serving him. there's no greater honor than in serving the demon lord.
Question: Is the hero the demon lord's archenemy?
Answer: Yes. The hero is the greatest impetus to the demon lord's plan. The hero must be killed over and over until the demon lord can take control of everything.
Question: How do you kill the hero over and over again? The hero can't be revived, can he?
Answer: Another hero will take the place of the fallen hero, hence the continuous cycle of slaying heroes is necessary.

Question: Where is this demon lord you speak of?
Answer: I don't know either. I've lost contact with my commanding officer ever since I got here. I'm now staying here thanks to your hospitality.
Question: Can you describe the demon lord?
Answer: That would be a difficult thing. Describing looks alone wouldn't be enough.
Question: Why does the demon lord want to take over the world?
Answer: I don't know, but I am glad to serve him.

Interview 16-3

Question: What is the name of the demon lord?
Answer: Makarov Quiscera.
Question: Is that his full name?
Answer: Yes, yes that is.
Question: What is the demon lord's gender?
Answer: It is male.

Interview 16-4

Question: How are you created?
Answer: I don't know that. I believe asking a necromancer would be more appropriate.
Question: Where can we find a necromancer?
Answer: I don't know that either.
Question: What is the demon lord's army composed of? Presuming there is one.
Answer: There's a lot. Undeads, demons, goblins, orcs, ogres, spiders, and many more.

Interview 16-5

Question: We've been calling you Abnormality-16. Do you have a name?
Answer: Frankly, no. Undeads are very numerous, so names aren't given to us. I've taken your nickname on me, Abnormality-16, as my true name.
Question: How many soldiers are in the demon lord's army?
Answer: I don't know. At least one hundred thousand, I think.
Question: What was your last order before you ended up here?
Answer: I've been ordered to survive and continue the fight. The hero must be killed.
Question: We're humans, but you're not attacking us. Why is that?
Answer: You're not the hero. Moreover, you've given me hospitality. It would be rude to attack someone who has welcomed you with open hands.

Interview 16-6

Answer: A necromancer did.
Question: What is the name of the necromancer?
Answer: Her name was Namiko Suri.
Question: When were you created?
Answer: About a few years ago. I don't remember when exactly.
Question: Did Namiko Suri make other skeletons?

Question: How many necromancers does the demon lord's army have?
Answer: Probably about a dozen.
Question: Why is it a small number?
Answer: Necromancers are actively hunted by humans, so their number is always small. Undeads are often hunted by humans as well. You're the first human I have ever met that don't attack undeads.

Origin

Abnormality-16 was found in a cave archeologists was digging. Abnormality-16 was friendly, but confused. Abnormality-16 did not know where the entity was. The unit was initially called to take the care of the entity, however, Abnormality-16 posed no threat unless provoked. The unit transported Abnormality-16 to the local government, but the local government refused to take custody of Abnormality-16. As a result, Abnormality-16 was instead given by the unit to the organization.
