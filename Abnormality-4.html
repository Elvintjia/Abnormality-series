Alias: Alan Smithee

Danger level: 0

Description

Abnormality-4 is a humanoid creature with brown skin wearing a suit and a hat. Abnormality-4 looks like a male human without genitals. Abnormality-4 does not have any facial features save for a wide mouth. Abnormality-4 almost always smiles. Abnormality-4 is capable of human language and is fluent in Spanish, English, mandarin, Japanese, and plenty of other languages. Abnormality-4 identifies himself as Alan Smithee. Abnormality-4 has absolute knowledge regarding everything about films. Abnormality-4 also knows the feeling of actors and production staff of a film before, during, and after the shooting. Abnormality-4 is friendly and docile, any staff members with interest in films are permitted to talk with Abnormality-4. Abnormality-4 produces no waste nor requires sustenance, but prefers to have something to eat. Abnormality-4 is allowed to roam the facility for as long as Abnormality-4 does not leave the facility or interfere with staff members' jobs.

Test logs

Test 4-1

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked about a film that is very old and barely known to today's people.

Result: Abnormality-4 could describe the film in great detail.

Test 4-2

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to write down everyone involved in the making of a film. Abnormality-4 was not allowed to watch the film or to be provided with the film's credits.

Result: Abnormality-4 could write down everyone involved in the making of the film, down to their contributions and how much screen time actors and extras get.

Test 4-3

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to tell about a film that has not yet been released.

Result: Abnormality-4 has no knowledge of the film, but after the release, Abnormality-4 knows everything about the film even without watching it.

Test 4-4

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to give his critique and opinion regarding a short movie script.

Result: Abnormality-4 gave constructive criticism. The director of the movie was given a chance to discuss the script with the entity by phone. The director heeded the entity's advice. The produced short movie is a huge success.

Test 4-5

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to direct a documentary film.

Result: Abnormality-4 had to do a lot of research and needed a lot of consultation with actual experts, but ultimately managed to make the movie. Staff member said the movie was excellent and informative.

Test 4-6

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to write an original movie script by himself.

Result: Abnormality-4 produced a movie script that does not resemble any move at the time of writing.

Test 4-7

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to watch a documentary film and tell which facts in the documentary film are wrong.

Result: Abnormality-4 is unable to which of the facts are true.

Test 4-8

Experiment: Abnormality-4 was asked to translate a movie with German dub.

Result: Abnormality-4 could translate the film with little difficulty.

Incidents

Incident 4-1

During filming, some filming equipment fell on Abnormality-4. The filming staff immediately lifted the equipment from Abnormality-4. Abnormality-4 was injured, but shed no blood. Abnormality-4 refused to be hospitalized and said that the accident won’t do much to him. Abnormality-4 was seen in good health and uninjured the next day. Abnormality-4 said the entity could recover from any injuries without medical attention and that the entity couldn’t feel pain.

Addenda

Addendum 4-1

After one interview, Abnormality-4 said he was getting bored of life in the facility, so he asked if he could be given a team to produce films as a hobby. After a series of debate, approval was given to Abnormality-4 to get a team of actors and film-makers to make a film. Abnormality-4 was given free reign as director, because Abnormality-4 is completely harmless. The film is still in progress. Film crew said that Abnormality-4 is a perfectionist, ordering a retake if even a small mistake has been made. However, Abnormality-4 is also said to be diligent and friendly, even helping the crew in mundane tasks whenever possible.

Addendum 4-2

Abnormality-4 finally completed the film. The film was shown to the organization's staff members. Staff members showered the film crew and the entity with praise for the great film. Staff members said they have never seen such a great film. As the film is produced by an abnormality, the film is not released to public and can only be watched by staff members. Currently, Abnormality-4 is provided with a crew of animators and voice actors to produce animated short films. Depending on the film, the film may be approved to be uploaded to the internet for the public to watch. Abnormality-4 is allowed to see comments and public reaction of his short films, but not allowed to communicate with them. Under Abnormality-4's request, the director name in the credits of the films are written under the name Alan Smithee.

Interview results

Interview 4-1

Question: What are you? Where do you come from?
Answer: I'm a creature born from humans' love of film and movies.
Question: How does that work?
Answer: I don't know that either.
Question: How do you know of films so well?
Answer: As a creature born of humans' love of film, I know everything about films. It is my raison d'etre.
Question: And how do you know about films you haven't watched?
Answer: Have you ever gotten an experience where you have a memory that others don't remember, or an idea you know not where it comes from? Try imagining it but with films.

Interview 4-2

Question: Where does the name Alan Smithee come from?
Answer: You humans created that name. I chose that name for myself befitting who I am.
Question: How do you require no food?
Answer: I am not technically a living thing, so I don't need them. They still taste delicious in my mouth, though.
Question: But you look like a living being.
Answer: I'm not too sure myself. I don't have reproductive organs and I don't have waste disposal. I am not born by simple biology.

Interview 4-3

Question: What are your views on modern film-making?
Answer: The modern world has an intriguing approach to film-making. Some uses films to deliver messages, some just for mere entertainments. Nothing they do is wrong. Films are meant to fulfill the desire of those who makes them, whether it is for entertainment, as a message, or simply as an achievement for the creators.
Question: Are there anything you dislike about films?
Answer: No not really. I enjoy every part of a film, no matter what it is.
Question: Who is your favourite film director?
Answer: I can't decide on that one. There are too many masterpieces in the world of films.

Interview 4-4

Question: Where did your suit come from?
Answer: I stole it. I used to be naked. It felt indecent, but I have no money or anything, so I had to steal.
Question: Would you prefer to have other kind of clothing?
Answer: No, I feel comfortable with my suit. I'd rather have it be washed and sewed back together than have it replaced.
Question: What about the hat?
Answer: Same as the suit.
Question: Are there any film you know not about?
Answer: Maybe. If there are, I wouldn't know that.

Interview 4-5

Question: Where did you first appear?
Answer: Pardon?
Question: As in, where were you born? Or, came to be? Which city?
Answer: Ah, I don't actually remember. It's been quite long.
Question: For how long have you been alive? Or, how old are you?
Answer: I think I am 15 years old. I don't remember my birth date. I only remember the year I came to be.

Interview 4-6

Question: Do you like documentary movies?
Answer: Of course I do! I'm not that knowledgeable when it comes to documentaries though.
Question: If you were asked to make a documentary, would you be able to?
Answer: Depending on the documentary you ask me to do, it might be possible, though I would have to do plenty of research.
Question: What kind of documentary movies would you be able to do?
Answer: I don't know that yet. I never tried that before, have I?
Question: Which topic would be easier for you to make a documentary about?
Answer: Probably showbiz or entertainment.

Origin

Abnormality-4 was first found in the USA. Abnormality-4 was seen leaving a movie theatre at the evening. Reports of the sighting came in, and commandos were sent to secure Abnormality-4. The next morning, Abnormality-4 was found in the city park feeding birds with breadcrumbs while talking to another person regarding the film he watched the previous evening. Abnormality-4 was willingly contained and brought to the facility.
