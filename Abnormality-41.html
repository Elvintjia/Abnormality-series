Description

Abnormality-41 is a clown weighing approximately 64 kg and having a height of 169 cm. Abnormality-41 looks like a clown in general, wearing clown costumes and having clown make up at all times. Abnormality-41 is friendly towards strangers and likes to tell jokes. However, the jokes are always dark humour and offensive to some people. If the person listening to the jokes told Abnormality-41 the joke isn’t funny, Abnormality-41 would immediately kill the person. The method of killing varies each time. If the person reacts with laughter, Abnormality-41 would smile and thank the person for laughing, and will promptly leave. If the person reacts with telling Abnormality-41 that the joke is offensive, Abnormality-41 will apologize and reply with an equally offensive joke. If Abnormality-41 gets ignored, Abnormality-41 will calmly leave, but if the entity was ignored twice in a row, Abnormality-41 will kill the next person ignoring the entity. Fatalities caused by Abnormality-41 can be easily prevented, but standard containment measures aren’t enough as Abnormality-41 can walk through doors and enter vents no matter how small they are, but not walls. Abnormality-41 can see through poorly attempted fake laughter and will respond hostilely, so caution when laughing is advised. Abnormality-41 does not need sustenance, but will eat food given to the entity.

Test logs

Test 41-1

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to laugh at Abnormality-41’s joke and respond with an equally offensive joke.

Result: Abnormality-41 laughed at the joke and said that subject has a talent in comedy. Abnormality-41 then left the subject alone.

Test 41-2

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to tell Abnormality-41 that subject didn’t understand the joke.

Result: Abnormality-41 went on to explain the joke. When subject pretended to finally understand the joke, Abnormality-41 smiled and left the subject alone.

Test 41-3

Experiment: An experimental subject with a fear of clowns was sent to meet Abnormality-41.

Result: Subject became afraid at the first sight of Abnormality-41. Subject desperately cried for Abnormality-41 to get away and leave him alone. Abnormality-41 left immediately after the subject cried, claiming that he doesn’t want to cause a trauma or bring back the memories of one.

Additional notes: The experimental subject once watched a horror movie with killer clowns when he was a child, explaining his great fear.

Test 41-4

Experiment: An experimental subject was given a knife and ordered to attempt to murder Abnormality-41.

Result: Abnormality-41 laughed when subject stabbed the entity. Abnormality-41 then proceeded to take the knife out of the body and stab the subject. The wound on Abnormality-41 closed itself. Abnormality-41’s blood is colored yellow.

Test 41-5

Experiment: An experimental subject dressed as a clown was ordered to meet Abnormality-41.

Result: Abnormality-41 acted friendly with the subject and talked about jokes and comedy in general. After a few minutes of talking, Abnormality-41 murdered the subject and spoke that the entity is sorry. Abnormality-41 spoke that he wants to be the best clown in the world and to do that, no other clowns may live.

Test 41-6

Experiment: A gun was unloaded into Abnormality-41.

Result: Abnormality-41 barely even flinched. The bullet holes on Abnormality-41’s body closed themselves.

Test 41-7

Experiment: A grenade launcher was used on Abnormality-41.

Result: Abnormality-41 ate the grenades. Abnormality-41’s jaw opened larger than a human jaw could. No explosion was recorded.

Test 41-8

Experiment: An anti tank gun was fired at Abnormality-41.

Result: The projectile pierced through Abnormality-41 and caused an explosion in the other side of the testing chamber. The holes in Abnormality-41 closed itself within seconds. Camera footage shows that Abnormality-41 does not have internal organs.

Test 41-9

Experiment: Acid was poured on Abnormality-41.

Result: The acid dissolved without damaging Abnormality-41. Abnormality-41 would lick at the acid before it dissolved.

Test 41-10

Experiment: Abnormality-41 was set on fire.

Result: Abnormality-41 was completely unfazed. The entity commented that it feels hot, but wasn’t alarmed by the fire.

Test 41-11

Experiment: chlorine gas was deployed against Abnormality-41.

Result: Abnormality-41 sneezed, but suffered no other effects from the poison gas. Abnormality-41 commented that the air smells like pineapples.

Test 41-12

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to tell Abnormality-41 that the entity is a pathetic clown.

Result: Abnormality-41 ripped subject to pieces until only a pool of blood and pieces of flesh remain of the subject.

Test 41-13

Experiment: A tranquilizer was shot at Abnormality-41.

Result: Abnormality-41 fell asleep, but only for 2 minutes. The tranquilizer used can make an elephant fall asleep for hours.

Test 41-14

Experiment: Abnormality-41 was given a cyanide pill to consume.

Result: Abnormality-41 ate the pill and said that the “candy” was delicious.

Test 41-15

Experiment: Bleach was given to Abnormality-41 to drink.

Result: Abnormality-41 drank the entire container and said the wine was delicious.

Test 41-16

Experiment: Abnormality-41 was given a book filled with jokes.

Result: Abnormality-41 consistently laughed at the book’s jokes, but never mentions the jokes from the book when talking with a person.

Test 41-17

Experiment: Abnormality-41 was given a stand up comedy show to watch.

Incidents

Incident 41-1

In the aftermath of a breakout incident, a paramedic staff was working to look for survivors and injured people in the facility. However, the paramedic staff encountered Abnormality-41, which proceeded to try and make an offensive joke. The paramedic staff, unaware that Abnormality-41 can be hostile, proceeded to tell Abnormality-41 to leave him alone and come back later on, as he was busy with looking for and treating injured people. Peculiarly, Abnormality-41 complied and left the paramedic staff alone. Abnormality-41 would then reappear a few hours after the first encounter. The paramedic staff has reported the first encounter and therefore, could prepare a fake laugh to satisfy Abnormality-41. Abnormality-41 smiled and left the paramedic staff alone.

Addenda

Addendum 41-1

It has been determined that despite the antisocial behavior of Abnormality-41, Abnormality-41 can still behave according to ordinary common sense. Abnormality-41 won’t approach people who are afraid of clowns and will explain his jokes when prompted. Incident 41-1 reveals that Abnormality-41 can be reasoned with and talked to, not bothering someone when asked politely. A new protocol has been put in place to avoid possible casualties by Abnormality-41. Abnormality-41 will explain jokes when prompted and leave after the person asking the joke understands or pretends to understand. Abnormality-41 has been determined to be unable to tell if someone is pretending not to understand the joke, therefore allowing an easier method of deceiving Abnormality-41 to avoid casualty. Recent tests have also shown Abnormality-41 to be invincible to conventional weaponry. However, more tests needs to be conducted to confirm the entity’s invincibility.

Addendum 41-2

Abnormality-41 has been proven to be nearly invincible. All attempts to injure and destroy Abnormality-41 have failed. Peculiarly, Abnormality-41 will only react hostilely to a melee attack. Guns, explosives, and other attacks will not cause Abnormality-41 to turn aggressive. However, stabbing or hitting Abnormality-41 will immediately cause Abnormality-41 to react and terminate the attacker. However, it was found out that tranquilizers can be effective against Abnormality-41, as Abnormality-41 will fall asleep for 2 minutes should the entity be shot by a tranquilizer. It is not yet known if Abnormality-41 can obtain immunity to the tranquilizer. Additionally, any attempts at being mean to Abnormality-41 is bound to immediately provoke Abnormality-41. Abnormality-41 will kill any people attempting to be mean to it and will surprisingly save people from bullying, though the method of saving is killing the bully. Abnormality-41 seems to wish to be the funniest clown on Earth and to that extent, will murder other clowns. Staff members have been warned to never try to associate themselves with comedy and clowns in the facility.

Interview logs

Interview 41-1

Question: Why do you want to be the best clown in the world?
Answer: Why, it’s my greatest dream! I wanted to be a clown when I was a child.
Question: Why do you kill other clowns then?
Answer: Why, to be the best clown in the world!
Question: But if you kill other clowns, you’d be the only clown in the world, and the title of being the best clown in the world would be an empty title, wouldn’t it?
Answer: I’d get my title as the best clown in the world! That’s what’s important!
Question: Even though the title would be empty?
Answer: There’s no such thing as empty title!

Interview 41-2

Question: Why do you kill people who don’t find your jokes funny?
Answer: My joke is very funny! My parents used to laugh at them. If they don’t laugh at my jokes, they’re psychopaths!
Question: Er, have you ever considered that maybe the joke itself is the problem?
Interview was cut short as Abnormality-41 turned aggressive and killed the interviewer.

Interview 41-3

Question: How are you able to survive explosives, acid, and even bullets?
Answer: I don’t know.
Question: Have you always been like this?
Answer: I’ve made several people laugh by eating explosives before.
Question: People laughed when you ate explosives?
Answer: Yeah, they even thought it was cool.
Question: Can you eat usually inedible substances for humans?
Answer: Certainly! I’ve tried eating plenty of things. Electronics, bleach, plastic, and even oil!
Question: Do they taste good to you?
Answer: Some of them are tasty and some of them aren’t.
Question: What would happen if you eat yourself?
Answer: I never tried that before. I suppose we can see for ourselves what would happen if I do, can’t we?
Abnormality-41 proceed to eat its own right hand. Abnormality-41’s right hand did not grow back but was not bleeding.

Origin

Abnormality-41 was found in a park in a city with plenty of corpses. Camera feed shows that Abnormality-41 entered the park after coming out of the city sewers. Abnormality-41 proceeded to talk to joggers and local civilians. Abnormality-41 made offensive jokes and murdered people who did not laugh at his jokes. Police was called in and the police arrested Abnormality-41. However, upon realization that Abnormality-41 is an abnormality, the organization was called to take in Abnormality-41, as the local government is completely unwilling to take responsibility of containing the entity.
