Description

Abnormality-30 is a room containing an invisible ghost. Whilst in Abnormality-30, the ghost cannot be detected by sight, but can be heard and communicated with. The ghost is friendly in the first two minutes after a person enters Abnormality-30, but will then become sinister. The ghost will bombard the person with disturbing thoughts and strange ideas. If the person continues hearing it, the person will become willing to do whatever the ghost tells him to do. People under the ghost's control is designated as Abnormality-30As. The ghost typically orders Abnormality-30A to vandalize, murder, or commit other disturbing actions. An Abnormality-30A will revert to a normal human, that is no longer under the ghost's control, if the Abnormality-30A is denied the ghost's voice for long periods of time. The entrance to Abnormality-30 must always be guarded by at least two sentinels to prevent unauthorized entry.

Test logs

Test 30-1

Experiment: Multiple experimental subjects were ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: Everyone claims to be able to hear the ghost's voice. However, what the ghost said to each of them were all different. The tone of voice was also different between each subject.

Test 30-2

Experiment: A deaf experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: The subject said he felt like he heard something, but was not sure if heard is quite the right word.

Test 30-3

Experiment: An experimental subject with earplugs was ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: The subject claimed to be able to hear a voice in her head.

Test 30-4

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-30 and engage on a conversation with the ghost. A listening device was strapped on the subject.

Result: The subject claimed to be able to speak with the ghost, but the listening device recorded only the subject's voice.

Test 30-5

Experiment: Communication with the ghost was attempted by speaker and microphone.

Result: No communication was possible with speaker and microphone.

Test 30-6

Experiment: A mute experimental subject was ordered to attempt communication with the ghost with writings.

Result: The subject said that the ghost claimed to be illiterate.

Test 30-7

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to attempt to communicate with the ghost by ASL.

Result: The subject said the ghost taunted and made death threats to him.

Test 30-8

Experiment: A mentally unstable experimental subject was ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: It took 6 minutes for the ghost to convince the subject to attempt murder. The attempted murder failed.

Test 30-9

Experiment: Abnormality-1 was ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: Abnormality-1 seemed nervous when entering, and exited Abnormality-30 exactly after 1 minutes and 59 seconds had passed.

Test 30-10

Experiment: An experimental subject was restrained and put inside Abnormality-30.

Result: After one hour, subject was taken out of the room. Subject was muttering nonsensical jumbles of words and could not reason or do anything. Subject committed suicide at the first opportunity available.

Test 30-11

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to continuously communicate with the ghost using ASL for 10 minutes.

Result: After 3 minutes of ASL communication, all recording devices within Abnormality-30 suddenly shut down. The doors closed and wouldn't open. All windows to the room became non-transparent. After a few minutes, the door opens, the recording devices reboots, and the windows became transparent. The subject was left dead inside with all of his inner organs missing.

Test 30-12

Experiment: An experimental subject with PTSD was ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: The subject seemed alleviated of his trauma initially, but became aggressive soon after. Sentinels executed the subject.

Test 30-13

Experiment: An experimental subject wearing Abnormality-31 was ordered to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: Subject was immediately under the ghost's control. Subject was executed due to aggressive behavior.

Test 30-14

Experiment: A priest with a crucifix was told to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: The priest did not hear any voice concerning the ghost.

Test 30-15

Experiment: A monk was told to enter Abnormality-30.

Result: The monk could not hear the ghost even after meditating inside Abnormality-30 for 10 minutes.

Incidents

Incident 30-1

A sentinel guarding Abnormality-30's entrance became aggressive and attacked several staff members. The sentinel was restrained and later came back to his sense. The sentinel said he remembers everything he had done but does not remember why he did so. The sentinel claimed to have heard the ghost, but ignored it. He then started feeling strange and eventually attempted to go on a rampage. Investigation if the ghost can communicate with humans outside of Abnormality-30 is underway. Peculiarly, The sentinel never used his gun when he attacked, despite carrying it.

Incident 30-2

A staff member in the facility reported hearing some voices talking to him. A psychiatrist was called in to check him, but nothing out of the ordinary was found. After gaining approval, the staff member was ordered to enter Abnormality-30. The staff member was ordered to exit within 30 seconds of entering. After his exit, the staff member claimed the voice he heard was exactly the same as the ghost.

Incident 30-3

The facility experienced some electrical problems and systematic malfunction. When investigated, staff member Jones was delirious and messing with the facility's mainframe and the electricity generator. Staff member Jones claimed to have heard some kind of voice. It was later confirmed that the voice was the ghost in Abnormality-30. Staff member Jones have messed with the mainframe and the electricity generator, but not much damage had been done.

Addenda

Addendum 30-1

It has been concluded that the ghost in Abnormality-30 can communicate and influence people outside of Abnormality-30. The ghost has yet to communicate with anyone outside the facility. It is unclear how far the ghost's communication range is from Abnormality-30. However, the ghost's frequency of communication with anyone outside of Abnormality-30 is very low and does not pose as a security problem. Nevertheless, any staff member on site having heard a voice out of the blue should immediately report it and get psychiatric assistance. Abnormality-30's danger level has been increased to 2. Additionally, any person manipulated by the ghost seems to be incapable of using modern technology.

Addendum 30-2

It has been determined that everyone will hear a different version of the ghost's voice. No two person will hear the exact same version of the ghost's voice. Deaf people can hear the ghost's voice. Earplugs have no effect on the ghost's voice. Electronics and devices cannot record the ghost's voice. The ghost seems to be illiterate and unable to read any writing. Using ASL to communicate with the ghost will anger the ghost and may cause death to the communicating person. A person with some mental illness such as PTSD can be influenced much more easily by the ghost. Staying inside Abnormality-30 will cause hostility, but staying even longer may cause suicidal tendencies.

Origin

Abnormality-30 was found in Ukraine. A group of teenagers entered an abandoned house as a test of guts. However, one of the teenagers entered Abnormality-30. Upon hearing the ghost, the teenager informed his friends and they all entered Abnormality-30. The incident resulted in 3 teenagers injured and 1 death amongst the teenagers. The police made investigations and local press was involved. After some covering up by the information department, Abnormality-30 was transported into the facility.
