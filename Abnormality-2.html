Description

Abnormality-2 consists of two things. A piece of paper and a hooded figure, respectively designated as Abnormality-2A and Abnormality-2B. Abnormality-2A is a piece of paper made from goat skin. On Abnormality-2A, a text is written. However, the text on Abnormality-2A is always perceived differently by each person. One person could see a Gandhi quote in the text, another could be reading a philosophical message written by Plato. No two person could see the same version of texts, even through photo or video. Abnormality-2B is a hooded figure that arrives in random time everyday to write on Abnormality-2A. Abnormality-2B wears a purple cloak that covers its entire body. Abnormality-2B has a black skin colour, with seemingly no hair and no facial features, almost like a mannequin. Abnormality-2B will always arrive through a black portal appearing near Abnormality-2A. During Abnormality-2B's presence, Abnormality-2A will always be seemingly blank. Abnormality-2B will proceed to conjure a quill out of thin air, and write something down on Abnormality-2A. Abnormality-2A will continue to be blank, even as Abnormality-2B writes upon it. after up to 5 minutes of writing, Abnormality-2B will then leave through the same black portal. The black portal will disappear, and texts of writings will appear on Abnormality-2A. Note that Abnormality-2 may seem harmless, but it is not. See incident 2-1 for explanation. Whenever Abnormality-2A is to be viewed, at least two personnel must view it at the same time and any out of character behavior must be reported. Personnel acting strangely must be sent to a psychiatrist for psychiatric assistance.

Test logs

Test 2-1

Experiment: An attempt in communication was made with Abnormality-2B.

Result: Abnormality-2B can communicate in English.

Test 2-2

Experiment: Communication with a variety of language was attempted with Abnormality-2B

Result: Abnormality-2B understands all spoken language and what Abnormality-2B says can always be understood by everyone, regardless of language difference. Video and audio recording yields similar results.

Test 2-3

Experiment: Two experimental subjects with two different mother tongue were asked to read Abnormality-2A

Result: Both subjects claimed the text was written in their own respective mother tongue.

Test 2-4

Experiment: An experimental subject was asked to attempt entering the black portal Abnormality-2B uses to enter and exit.

Result: Experimental subject could not enter the portal due to an invisible barrier blocking him, although Abnormality-2B can enter the portal with ease.

Test 2-5

Experiment: An experimental subject was asked to write something down on Abnormality-2A

Result: Nothing changed on Abnormality-2A despite the experimental subject repeatedly trying to write something.

Test 2-6

Experiment: Abnormality-2A was put on a very high platform out of reach. Abnormality-2B's actions were observed.

Result: Abnormality-2B floated to the platform to write on Abnormality-2A. Method of floating is still unknown. Abnormality-2B proceeded to land on the ground and reenter the black portal he came from.

Test 2-7

Experiment: Abnormality-2A was put inside a thick safe. How Abnormality-2B would write on Abnormality-2A was observed.

Result: Camera feed inside the safe shows that a black portal opened inside the safe and Abnormality-2B's hand emerged from the portal. Abnormality-2B's hand conjured a quill and wrote on the Abnormality-2A before pulling his hand back. Portal then disappeared and the text on Abnormality-2A changed.

Test 2-8

Experiment: Several personnel attempted to stop Abnormality-2B from writing on Abnormality-2A at all costs.

Result: Gone horribly wrong. See incident 2-2.

Test 2-9

Experiment: Abnormality-2A was to be burnt.

Result: Cancelled. Fear of repeat of incident 2-2 caused planned destructive experiments to be rejected.

Test 2-10

Experiment: Paint was poured on Abnormality-2A.

Result: The paint passed through Abnormality-2A and fell to the table the entity was on.

Test 2-11

Experiment: Water was poured on Abnormality-2A.

Result: Abnormality-2A became wet, but became dry soon after.

Test 2-12

Experiment: Abnormality-2A was submerged in water.

Result: Abnormality-2B took Abnormality-2A out of water to write on it, and then proceeded to put the entity back in the water before leaving.

Incidents

Incident 2-1

Staff member Ferdinand read what was written on Abnormality-2A. Afterwards, staff member Ferdinand let out a creepy and strange laugh. Staff member Ferdinand then acted strangely out of character and then proceeded to commit suicide not long after. It was unknown what staff member Ferdinand had read, but it is speculated to have something in connection a trauma he has. Safety protocols have been enacted to prevent similar occurrences.

Incident 2-2

In test 2-8, staff members attempt to stop Abnormality-2B from writing on Abnormality-2B. Sentinels were watching the experiment in case something unexpected happens. Abnormality-2B appeared and approached Abnormality-2A. Staff members tried distracting or even convincing Abnormality-2B not to write on Abnormality-2A. Abnormality-2B ignored them and tried to get to Abnormality-2A. Staff members blocked Abnormality-2B's way. Abnormality-2B then politely asked staff members to step out of the way and let him through. The staff member refused. Abnormality-2B then became aggressive and pulled out a knife, stabbing staff members blocking the way. Sentinels immediately interfered. In the ensuing firefight, Abnormality-2B was shot several times and was terminated. Abnormality-2B's body dropped after being shot twice and spilled no blood. Abnormality-2B's body disappeared over a period of 2 minutes, and so did the black portal. After the portal and Abnormality-2B's body disappear, every personnel involved saw that the text on Abnormality-2A written as "You will regret that." Personnel uninvolved in the incident saw the Abnormality-2A as blank, with nothing written. At midnight, all personnel involved in the test had a heart attack simultaneously. No one survived.

Incident 2-3

Staff member named Boris attempted to invite Abnormality-2B to tea after the entity has written in Abnormality-2A. Abnormality-2B accepted the invitation and went to drink tea with staff member Boris. Abnormality-2B was capable of smelling and drinking tea, though facial features like a mouth are not present. Abnormality-2B remarked that the tea of our world tastes much better than tea in the entities world. Staff member Boris talked with Abnormality-2B about various things. Unfortunately, nothing of the conversation has been recorded. Eventually, Abnormality-2B finished his tea and left. Before leaving, Abnormality-2B thanked staff member Boris and said he would happily come to drink tea again.

Incident 2-4

A group of staff members have conducted a forbidden test on Abnormality-2. An experimental subject was ordered to shoot Abnormality-2B. Afterwards, everyone involved in the scheme escaped using an aeroplane to a different time zone. The goal of the test was to see what would happen if anyone involved in the incident were to flee to another time zone and hence never got to midnight. Every single staff member involved died due to heart attack simultaneously when the local time of Abnormality-2A's location reach midnight. After the incident, security level of Abnormality-2 is increased to avoid forbidden experiments from being conducted again in the future.

Addenda

Addendum 2-1

After it was found out that the Abnormality-2B is capable of speech, interviews have been conducted to find out more regarding Abnormality-2B. Additionally, after incident 2-2, all test that involves brute force or anything close to provoking the entities are strictly forbidden. All personnel are forbidden from doing anything to hinder Abnormality-2B or harm Abnormality-2. Personnel that attempts to do so is grounds for punishments.

Addendum 2-2

After incident 2-3, Abnormality-2B regularly drinks tea with staff members after writing on Abnormality-2A. Staff members must invite Abnormality-2B to tea when he appears and are encouraged to chat with Abnormality-2B to get information, though it is warned that staff members should not ask sensitive questions or enforce opinions. Abnormality-2A's containment chamber is to be furnished with a table and some chairs. Tea must be prepared at all times for in case that Abnormality-2B arrive. At least one staff member must always watch over Abnormality-2A. All conversations with Abnormality-2B is to be recorded. Abnormality-2B will normally chat and drink tea for up to twenty minutes before thanking staff members and leaving.

Interview results

Interview 2-1

Question: Who or what are you?
Answer: I am simply a messenger.
Question: From who are you a messenger?
Answer: I am a messenger from a creature unknown to you, and existence that will drive insanity to the minds of those who knows.
Question: Can you please describe this creature you are a messenger of?
Answer: I refuse to. People driven insane by the knowledge can never be cured.
Question: Why are you writing as a messenger?
Answer: It's my duty.
Question: Can this duty of yours be neglected?
Answer: Yes, but I don't want to neglect it.
Question: Why?
Answer: I'd rather not tell you.

Interview 2-2

Question: What are you writing?
Answer: A message I was instructed to write.
Question: Why does everyone reads different versions of the message you're writing?
Answer: I do not know.
Question: What is that portal you enter and leave from?
Answer: It is a tool lent to me by whom I am a messenger from.
Question: How does it work?
Answer: That is not for me to know.
Question: What is beyond the portal?
Answer: A world that a regular human mind will not understand. I refuse to describe it.

Interview 2-3

Question: What are your thoughts on the previous incident?
Answer: Which one? The one where some of you stabbed me?
Question: Yes, we would like to apologize to you for it.
Answer: It is not of your concern. The punishment has been given out to the perpetrators. Uninvolved people have no need to apologize.
Question: Are you still the same one as the one who was stabbed?
Answer: Yes, yes I am. Messengers are replaced when after centuries of service, not by death.
Question: What happens after you die? Are you revived by this being you are a messenger from?
Answer: I wake up in the other world, body intact with no injuries. I will return to do my duties after that.
Question: Do you have anything else to do besides your duty? A personal life maybe?
Answer: I am forbidden from answering that.
Question: Why? And by whom is it forbidden?
Answer: Why it is forbidden is also forbidden for me to answer. It is forbidden by the rules of my duty.

Interview 2-4

Question: How do you like the tea?
Answer: The tea of this world tastes magnificent.
Question: Is it that good?
Answer: Well, if compared to the tea of the other world.
Question: How does the tea of the other world tastes?
Answer: It's plain and bitter. It has no aroma of this world's tea either.
Question: Could you try bringing tea from your world to ours?
Answer: Absolutely not. Humans are incapable of digesting food from the other world.
Question: Is it lethal for humans?
Answer: No, but I heard it requires extensive medical attention to save whoever eats food from the other world.
Question: Do you have no problem consuming tea like that? Is it safe for you?
Answer: Aside from the bad taste, it's okay for us. I very much prefer having the tea of this world though.

Interview 2-5

Question: Why don't you bring home some of the food? We don't mind letting you take it.
Answer: No, no, I'm not allowed to bring anything from this world to the other world.
Question: Why is that? You seem to be okay with eating our food though.
Answer: Consuming or using whatever is available in this world is allowed, but bringing things back things form this world to the other world is forbidden.
Question: So, why's that?
Answer: It's like bringing foreign objects home. We don't know much about it and we don't know what effect it will bring to our society, so we may not bring anything back home.
Question: Are you allowed to tell people from your world about what you do here?
Answer: No, that kind of information may also have a big effect on our society, so that too is forbidden.

Origin

Abnormality-2 was found in one of the cult's outpost. Watchmen reported its location and commandos were sent to raid it. A firefight ensued. Due to the commandos superior training and technologically advanced equipment, the cult members were wiped out. Those that couldn't participate in combat either chose suicide or fleeing. Abnormality-2 was found within one of the rooms of the outpost. Abnormality-2 was first thought to be an ancient manuscript, however it was quickly proven as an abnormality. Abnormality-2 was then brought back to the organization's facility.
