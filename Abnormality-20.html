Alias: Position switch safe

Danger level: 1

Description

Abnormality-20 is a giant 3 by 3 meter cubic safe colored black. Abnormality-20 has a rough thickness of 1.5 cm. Abnormality-20 is made from steel that is of very high quality. Abnormality-20 is very durable and will repair itself after being damaged. The method of repair is still unidentified. If an object is put within Abnormality-20 and Abnormality-20 is closed, the object put inside Abnormality-20 will switch position with a random object on Earth. There are no clear pattern as to what object will be switched if a certain object is put inside Abnormality-20. All objects that is switched will always be small enough to fit into Abnormality-20. Abnormality-20 can never be locked, despite being a safe. Abnormality-20 is extremely heavy, weighing 40 kg while being empty.

Test logs

Test 20-1

Experiment: A bottle of water was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: Upon closing and opening, a clock was found inside Abnormality-20.

Test 20-2

Experiment: A monkey was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: Upon closing and opening, a pool of blood was found inside the safe. Nothing else was in Abnormality-20.

Test 20-3

Experiment: An experimental subject with a bug to detect location was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: Upon closing Abnormality-20, the bug was deactivated and upon opening Abnormality-20, only a pool of blood was left.

Test 20-4

Experiment: A non-living object with a bug was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: The bug could be tracked even after the non-living object gets teleported to another part of the planet.

Test 20-5

Experiment: An egg was put inside Abnormality-20. A bug was stuck to the egg.

Result: Upon closing and opening, the egg was teleported. The bug was still active. The egg had been cracked open by the time a watchman found the egg.

Test 20-6

Experiment: A candle was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: Upon closing and opening the door, a book was found in Abnormality-20.

Test 20-7

Experiment: A leaf was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: The leaf was teleported to Europe and a pencil replaced it.

Test 20-8

Experiment: A plant was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: The plant was teleported to Russia. A ball took its place in Abnormality-20.

Test 20-9

Experiment: A dead fish was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: The dead fish was teleported elsewhere and a chair took its place in the safe.

Test 20-10

Experiment: A bread with mold was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: The bread was teleported intact with the mold.

Test 20-11

Experiment: A fruit was put in Abnormality-20.

Result: The fruit was teleported entirely intact.

Test 20-12

Experiment: Two objects were put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: After closing and opening, the object inside Abnormality-20 was each replaced with different objects. Both objects have been determined not to be from the same location.

Test 20-13

Experiment: A bucket of water was put inside Abnormality-20.

Result: The bucket of water was transported along the water put inside.

Incidents

Incident 20-1

Abnormality-20 suddenly disappeared from containment room. Camera feeds showed Abnormality-20 disappearing instantly without warning. Investigation to search for the whereabouts of Abnormality-20 was conducted. After a while, Abnormality-20 was found in a remote village in Africa. Abnormality-20 was recovered and information regarding the entity was covered up. Investigation to discover the trigger for Abnormality-20's disappearance is ongoing.

Incident 20-2

It was found that the candle from test 20-6 was transported to a library and caused a great fire. Most of the books in the library were burnt and two people died. Dozens others were injured. Watchmen had to plant fake evidences to hide the possibility of the public finding out about the abnormalities. Possibly dangerous objects are no longer allowed to be put in Abnormality-20.

Addenda

Addendum 20-1

It has been determined by investigation that the cause of incident 20-1 was caused due to lack of use of Abnormality-20 for a week. If Abnormality-20 isn't used for a week, the entity will teleport itself to a random location in the world. Therefore, Abnormality-20 must be used at least once every week. Additionally, in light of incident 20-2, no potentially dangerous object may be put inside Abnormality-20. Lastly, it has been found out that any animals put inside Abnormality-20 will disappear while only leaving a pool of blood. Where they go is unknown and has yet to be identified.
