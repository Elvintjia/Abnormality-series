Abnormality-12

Alias: Encyclopedia of thought

Danger level: 0

  3. Incidents

Description

Abnormality-12 is an encyclopedia filled with ideology, beliefs, religion, thought, and the likes. Abnormality-12 can influence anyone reading to either dislike or like the idea read. If a person reads a chapter describing an ideology they think is bad, the person will come to like the idea. If a person reads a chapter describing an idea they think is good, the person will come to hate the idea. The effects are not reversible without Abnormality-12. So far, no person has managed to be unaffected by Abnormality-12 after reading the entity.

Test logs

Test 12-1

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to read Abnormality-12. The amount of time of Abnormality-12's effects were noted.

Result: Abnormality-12's effects has not dissipated form subject until the writing of the test report.

Test 12-2

Experiment: A young boy with no concept of belief was told to read Abnormality-12.

Result: The boy could read Abnormality-12 but could not understand the entity's contents. No change in the boy was recorded.

Test 12-3

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to read a specific chapter of Abnormality-12 multiple times.

Result: The subject changed belief several times. Change in beliefs were consistent.

Test 12-4

Experiment: An ultranationalist was asked to read Abnormality-12.

Result: The ultranationalist suddenly chanted for revolution of his country. After being asked to read again, the ultranationalist reverted to his original view.

Test 12-5

Experiment: A depressed person was asked to read Abnormality-12

Result: No change in said person's depression.

Additional notes: Abnormality-12 only changes beliefs. Emotions are not affected.

Test 12-6

Experiment: A mentally insane person was ordered to read Abnormality-12.

Result: No effect on the insane person's sanity.

Additional notes: Reminder to researchers, Abnormality-12 only affects beliefs. Emotion and sanity are completely uninfluenced by Abnormality-12.

Test 12-7

Experiment: A devout Christian read a chapter in Abnormality-12 regarding Buddhism.

Result: Said person became a Buddhist. upon reading a chapter regarding Christianity, the person reverted to Christianity.

Test 12-8

Experiment: A person with neutral political belief was ordered to read Abnormality-12. It was then attempted to change the person's political belief to return to neutral.

Result: All attempts to revert the person's political belief failed. Not even Abnormality-12 was able to revert the person's belief back to being neutral.

Test 12-9

Experiment: A doodle was drawn with a pencil in one of Abnormality-12’s pages.

Result: Nothing happened. The doodle stayed as it is.

Test 12-9A

Experiment: The page of the drawn doodle was read by an experimental subject.

Result: The subject did not change her belief.

Test 12-9B

Experiment: The doodle was erased, then an experimental subject was ordered to read the page.

Result: The subject’s beliefs changed.

Incidents

Incident 12-1

An uncooperative experimental subject tore a page out of Abnormality-12. The page was glued back to Abnormality-12, but when another experimental subject read the previously torn page, no effect on the subject's beliefs were recorded. It has been theorized that torn pages from Abnormality-12 will lose its ability to influence a person's beliefs. Unfortunately, permission for testing has been repeatedly declined.

Addenda

Addendum 12-1

Abnormality-12 can and will only influence a person's beliefs. Religion and ideological beliefs can be changed by Abnormality-12, however sanity and emotional will stay uninfluenced. Abnormality-12's influence are life-long and will never dissipate. People with no concept of beliefs, such as the young or mentally impaired, cannot be influenced by Abnormality-12. Additionally, if pages that are torn from Abnormality-12 are read, the reading person will be uninfluenced.

Origin

Abnormality-12 was found in an abandoned agency facility. The agency abandoned the facility after a breakout incident occurring and opted to quarantine the location. After the threat has been neutralized, the agency chose to move the personnel and material elsewhere because the facility is too damaged to be operational and repairs would be too expensive. After that, commandos entered the facility to investigate available information. Most documents have either been destroyed, or brought back with the agency. However, some documents and Abnormality-12 was left behind, because the room which was their location was blocked off by debris and required heavy equipment to remove. The agency are unaware that the organization holds this abnormality, and it should be kept secret as much as possible.
