Abnormality-37

Alias: Selective acid

Danger level: 1

Description

Abnormality-37 is a cyan colored liquid of unknown composition. Abnormality-37 is acidic and corrosive. However, Abnormality-37’s corrosive effects only applies to inanimate objects. Any inanimate objects, regardless of what it is, will be melted by Abnormality-37. However, any living things that come in contact with Abnormality-37 will not be affected by Abnormality-37’s corrosive effects. The only way a person could die due to Abnormality-37 is by drowning inside Abnormality-37. Due to Abnormality-37’s nature of corroding any object it comes into contact with, Abnormality-37 is very difficult to transport to a different location. So far, the only object that could hold Abnormality-37 is a 5 meter square bathtub designated as Abnormality-37A. Abnormality-37A is capable of holding Abnormality-37 despite being an inanimate object, suffering no corrosion even after prolonged exposure. Research indicates Abnormality-37A to be a regular big bathtub. There are approximately 4 meter square of Abnormality-37 contained within Abnormality-37A. Abnormality-37 has a density of 0.5 kilogram per meter square. Abnormality-37 is determined to be acidic, but it is impossible to determine the entity’s exact ph.

Test logs

Test 37-1

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to drink a small portion of Abnormality-37.

Result: No change was recorded in subject. Abnormality-37 could not be processed by the subject’s digestive tract and was excreted as it is.

Test 37-2

Experiment: An experimental subject was ordered to swim in Abnormality-37.

Result: Subject’s clothes disintegrated. However, subject could swim for an indefinite amount of time with no repercussions.

Test 37-3

Experiment: A recently cut hair of a staff member was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The hair disintegrated as soon as it touched Abnormality-37.

Test 37-4

Experiment: Finger nails were dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The nails disintegrated immediately.

Test 37-5

Experiment: A monkey was thrown into Abnormality-37.

Result: The monkey died due to drowning. Its body disintegrated several seconds after drowning.

Test 37-6

Experiment: A bone was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The bone disintegrated.

Test 37-7

Experiment: Feces was dropped into Abnormality-37

Result: The feces disintegrated.

Test 37-8

Experiment: Enzyme was poured into Abnormality-37.

Result: The enzyme disintegrated. No protein could be detected in Abnormality-37.

Test 37-9

Experiment: An airtight tube filled with carbon dioxide was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The tube disintegrated. The carbon dioxide then disintegrated.

Test 37-10

Experiment: Water was poured into Abnormality-37.

Result: The water disintegrated. No sign of water could be found in Abnormality-37.

Test 37-11

Experiment: A plant was dropped in Abnormality-37.

Result: The plant was not disintegrated immediately, but if left alone will die and disintegrate.

Test 37-12

Experiment: A crab’s shell was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The shell disintegrated.

Test 37-13

Experiment: A burning torch was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The torch was extinguished immediately and the remains disintegrated.

Test 37-14

Experiment: A fire extinguisher was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The fire extinguisher disintegrated. The contents were also disintegrated.

Test 37-15

Experiment: Leather was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The leather disintegrated.

Test 37-16

Experiment: Organs were dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The organs did not disintegrate immediately. However, if left in Abnormality-37 for an extended period of time, the organs will disintegrate.

Test 37-17

Experiment: A pack of donated blood was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The pack holding the blood together disintegrated, but the blood did not immediately disintegrate. The blood scattered and disintegrated several minutes after.

Test 37-18

Experiment: An active flame thrower was dropped into Abnormality-37.

Result: The flame thrower was extinguished and then disintegrated.

Addenda

Addendum 37-1

It has been determined that drinking Abnormality-37 has no harmful effects. The human body is unable to process Abnormality-37. As such, Abnormality-37 will be excreted as it is with feces and urine. Additionally, it was noticed that a person’s body hair and nails will not disintegrate when swimming in Abnormality-37, yet cut nails and hair will disintegrate in Abnormality-37. Animals are also shown to be immune to Abnormality-37’s effects, with the only possibility of dying caused by drowning. Biological compounds separated from a living thing such as feces, bones, and enzymes are also prone to disintegration to Abnormality-37.

Addendum 37-2

Anything that is not Abnormality-37 itself will be disintegrated by Abnormality-37. Gas and liquid objects are not immune to disintegration by Abnormality-37. If fire was put into Abnormality-37, the fire will immediately be extinguished and the burning material immediately disintegrated. Body organs and blood will not immediately disintegrate upon contact with Abnormality-37, but will disintegrate after prolonged contact. Additionally, it has been noticed that Abnormality-37A is fairly weak and fragile despite being able to withstand Abnormality-37’s corrosive effects. There is no known method to repair Abnormality-37A, so caution must be enforced to prevent the only possible container for Abnormality-37 from breaking. Abnormality-37’s danger level has been changed from 0 to 1.

Origin

Abnormality-37 was found in the Underground Research Institution in Africa. Abnormality-37 and Abnormality-37A was left untouched in one of the chambers in the institution. Documents regarding Abnormality-37 was left, but not much was written about Abnormality-37. Due to the difficulties of transporting Abnormality-37, the entity has not been moved to the facility. A small outpost in the institution has been erected to monitor Abnormality-37. Testing must all be done in the institution where the entity is located.
